How it works

1. Calibrate your scale in the Calibrate tab.

2. Draw routes with the Pen tool (multi-point lines).

3. Select lines and click Measure. Labels appear as pills at the midpoint.

4. Drag labels freely — position is kept unless the line shape changes.

5. Moving a line to a different frame auto-recalculates with that frame's scale.

Calibrate scale

Select a vector line drawn over a known distance, enter the real length, choose where to save, then click Calculate.
